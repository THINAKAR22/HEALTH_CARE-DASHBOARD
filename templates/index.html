About HealthWatch

Our Mission

HealthWatch provides public health officials and policymakers with real-time disease surveillance data and AI-powered outbreak predictions to enable faster, more informed responses to emerging health threats.

How It Works

Data is collected from healthcare facilities, processed through our ML pipeline, and visualized on an interactive dashboard. Administrators can upload new data, while the system automatically updates predictions.
